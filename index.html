Welcome to the Tardif Neuroimaging Lab!

Welcome to the Tardif Neuroimaging lab: a magnetic resonance imaging lab of brain tissue microstructure. The lab is headed by Dr Christine Tardif, Assistant Professor in the Department of Biomedical Engineering and the Department of Neurology and Neurosurgery at McGill. We are part of the McConnell Brain Imaging Centre at the Montreal Neurological Institute, McGill University.
